Create a short animated promo scene for an open-source product called RanchMind.

Visual concept:
- three distinct roles, not a merged creature
- a red lobster as the execution specialist
- a white horse as the scheduler and dispatcher
- a calm humanoid operator as the memory and control plane

- retro-futurist operations barn at night
- the lobster works across multiple terminal screens with code, shell output, and task receipts
- the horse stands at a dispatch wall with clocks, message routes, and task queues
- the human stands in front of a glowing memory graph and dashboard
- threads of light connect all three stations into a single loop

Tone:
- open-source
- capable
- slightly playful
- cinematic but not childish

Do not create a monster hybrid.
Keep the three roles separate and readable.

On-screen text:
- RanchMind
- Lobster + Horse + Human
- Execute. Dispatch. Remember.

Style:
- clean motion-graphics look
- subtle neon accents
- smooth camera movement
- 8 to 12 seconds
- suitable as a GitHub launch promo
